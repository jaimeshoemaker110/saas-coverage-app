💡 Pro Tip

SaaS Consumption is a quantity of licenses in use metric - it is not revenue/signings. For example, it is the number of App Points in use for Maximo Application Suite, or number of Resource Units for watsonx.ai. Achievement is the sum of quantities in use for each eligible product that you can see on the product usage tab in ISC. Learn how to do that with quick video tips: https://yourlearning.ibm.com/activity/PLAN-75EBE64D32E8
